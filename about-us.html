The Story So Far…

Our story began in 2010, when actor and performer Tommy Balaam, fresh from graduating East 15 Acting School, discovered his passion for creating extraordinary children’s entertainment experiences. With a spark of inspiration and a heart full of adventure, Captain Fantastic was born — a superhero on a mission to rescue parties from the ordinary!

Since then, Captain Fantastic has become a nationwide success with over 60 entertainers, 11 UK franchises, and even an international presence in the USA. Alongside thrilling kids’ parties, we’ve launched mindfulness & wellbeing courses, a popular YouTube series, children’s books, and music albums.
