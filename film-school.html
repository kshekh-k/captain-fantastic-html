Film School!

Turn your child’s wildest ideas into reality at Captain Fantastic’s Film School, an exciting new after-school film-making club!

Guided by our professional entertainers, kids will explore directing, performing, and producing their own short films – from thrilling adventures to laugh-out-loud comedies. It’s a fantastic opportunity to get creative, learn new skills, and have loads of fun while making new friends!

Why Choose Captain Fantastic’s Film School?

Make Movies, Not Just Watch Them!

Kids won’t just watch the magic – they’ll create it! From storyboarding and acting to filming and editing, they’ll gain hands-on experience making their very own short films.

Teamwork Makes the Dream Work!

Teamwork Makes the Dream Work! Behind every great movie is an amazing team. Whether playing the lead role, directing, or operating the camera, every child works together to create something spectacular.

Learn, Create & Have FUN!

Our unique sessions combine film-making challenges, acting workshops, and creative games, ensuring kids are always inspired, engaged, and entertained while building lifelong memories.

Build Your Story, Week by Week!

Each week, children will explore creating fun characters, developing engaging scenes, and discovering film-making basics like simple special effects, stunts, and editing. By the end of the term, they’ll showcase their work in a celebratory screening event for friends and family!
