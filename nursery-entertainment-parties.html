Weekly Toddler Sessions

Early Years Fun & Learning

Make learning magical! Our weekly toddler sessions are designed around the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework, helping young children develop communication skills, social interaction, physical development, and imagination through songs, puppets, magic tricks, parachute games, and themed activities.

What’s Included in Our Weekly Toddler Sessions:

Music, Songs & Rhymes

Puppets & Interactive

Meet our lovable puppets and enjoy story-driven adventures that spark imagination and language development. Children will laugh, learn, and interact as they become part of the story!

Magic & Wonder

Our toddler-friendly magic tricks introduce children to science-based learning while keeping them entertained. Surprising tricks encourage problem-solving, curiosity, and focus.
